Horizontal & Bubbles

GTribe is a Ecosystem kind of thing. All of its people are at same level and each have equal contributation to the GCC. We also introduce the Bubble Workspace which is mean to transparency.

As in bubbles we can see the inner things. In a Bubble Workspace each team's work, progress, problems all are visible to entire people of GTribe, Hence anyone from GTribe can know what work is going on and hence able to sovle problem of different teams. The power of social computing motivate us to establish such transperent and integrated Workspace

Anyone & Anything

Knowing importance of Social Computing we want to build a culture of Openwork. Hence Anyone can do Anything in our GTribe culture.

Since GTribe contains people from various background, Hence it has many problems from different background. Therefore we introduce OpenWork where there are many problems from different backgrounds e,g: designs, marketing, Tech are available and anyone irrespective of their position can do these work to get different experiences or to do it for hobby.

Mental Models & Thinking

GTribe is a Human Kind of thing which has its own Mental Model and also has its own Thinker to use thses Mental Models to solve problems.

In GTribe we have Readers who reads many books, Journal etc, and build the mental model of GTribe. Mental Models of GTribe means all its people has the same shareable mental models. And specifically Thinker of GTribe use these prebuilt mental model to solve problem face by their respective bubbles.
